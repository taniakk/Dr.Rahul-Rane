CONSULTANT ORTHOPEDIC SURGEON

Dr. Rahul Rane

Dr. Rahul Rane is a highly skilled orthopedic specialist who has practiced in the field for over 16 years. He completed his Master of Orthopaedics (MS Ortho) at the prestigious Lokmanya Tilak Medical College, Mumbai, one of the largest trauma centers in Mumbai and Western India.

He was also awarded the Diplomate of the National Board (DNB Ortho) with distinction. Dr. Rahul completed advanced fellowship training in spine surgery in South Korea. He has authored chapters and published research papers in international journals. He specializes in joint replacement, musculoskeletal injuries, fractures, and minimally invasive spine surgery.

He enjoys running marathons and is a fitness enthusiast with a strong commitment to helping his patients recover faster.
