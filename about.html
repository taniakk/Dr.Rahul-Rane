About Dr. Rahul Rane

Dedicated to world-class orthopedic and spine care with compassion, precision, and a patient-first philosophy.

Orthopedic & Spine Surgeon

Dr. Rahul Rane

Dr. Rahul Rane is a talented orthopedic specialist who has worked in the area for over 16 years. He completed his Master of Orthopaedics (MS Ortho) from the prestigious Lokmanya Tilak Medical College in Mumbai — the largest trauma center in Mumbai and Western India.

Additionally, he was awarded a Diplomat of National Board (DNB Ortho) with distinction. South Korea provided Dr. Rahul with advanced fellowship training in spine surgery. He has chapters and research papers published in international journals. He specializes in joint replacement, musculoskeletal injuries, fractures, and minimally invasive spine surgery.

An avid marathon runner and fitness enthusiast, he believes in accelerated recovery. His expertise spans joint replacement, spine surgery, sports injuries, fractures, and complex trauma. His slogan: "My patient should resume to routine earliest."

Our Philosophy

Patient-First Approach

Dr. Rahul Rane is a highly respected orthopedic and spine surgeon with over 16 years of experience in treating complex bone, joint, and spine conditions. With a mission to deliver world-class orthopedic care with compassion and precision, he has become a trusted name for patients seeking Orthopedic Care near Powai and across Mumbai.

After completing his MS in Orthopaedics and DNB in Orthopaedic Surgery, Dr. Rane pursued a prestigious fellowship in spine surgery from South Korea, where he trained in advanced endoscopic and minimally invasive techniques. His practice blends modern technology with a deep understanding of musculoskeletal health.

Dr. Rane's areas of expertise include spine surgery, joint replacement, sports injury management, fracture care, arthroscopy, stem cell therapy, and height increase procedures. His goal is to restore mobility and relieve pain with safe and effective solutions.

At our clinic, every treatment plan begins with a detailed diagnosis, followed by clear communication, non-surgical options wherever possible, and complete support during recovery.
