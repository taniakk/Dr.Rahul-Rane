Our Branches

Expert orthopedic care, conveniently located across Mumbai. Find a branch near you.

At Dr. Rahul Rane’s Orthopaedic & Spine Care Clinic, our mission is to make high-quality orthopedic care easily accessible to patients across Mumbai. With multiple branches spread throughout the city, we offer expert consultation, diagnosis, and treatment at your convenience. Our network is recognized among the Best Orthopedic Clinics In Mumbai for its commitment to advanced care and consistent patient satisfaction.

We currently operate from several key locations, including Powai, Andheri East, Andheri West, Vile Parle, Chembur, and more. Dr. Rane is affiliated with top hospitals such as Hiranandani Hospital, Nanavati Max, Hinduja Healthcare, Criticare Hospital, Raksha Hospital, and Seven Hills Hospital. Each branch provides a full range of services—from consultation and diagnostics to surgery and post-treatment rehabilitation.
